mtn season of surprises _ y'ello santa

MTN season of surprises is an annual end of year event, where we give back to Nigerians. This year MTN customers will have the oppourtinity to get a gift such as free data, jumia shopping vouchers to purchase curated festive hampers, and mobile devices courtesy MTN, Coca-Cola, Golden penny, Maggi, and TECNO.

You will have the option to refer a loved to receive gifts via the new myMTN NG App, and the USSD channel (*135#), and a gift will sent to you and your loved one.

HOW IT WORKS

There are 2 easy ways to do this

Have a smartphone? Awesome

  • Fill in your MTN number below.

  • Click on the claim  button and a gift will be sent to you follow instructions.

    • No smartphone? NO worries

  • Dial *135#, Follow the prompt and put in your MTN number

  • A gift will be sent to you.
